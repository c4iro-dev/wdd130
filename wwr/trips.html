Whispering Falls

Perfect for first-timers and families, Whispering Falls takes you through lush pine forests and peaceful bends before cascading over a series of small, playful rapids. You’ll float past wildlife, sparkling waterfalls, and canyon walls that echo every laugh. A relaxing adventure with just the right splash of excitement.

Serpent's Run

Serpent’s Run offers a thrilling mid-level ride full of winding channels, fast drops, and quick paddling challenges. Ideal for those who’ve rafted before or love an active adventure, this trip combines action-packed rapids with jaw-dropping views of red-rock cliffs and hidden alcoves along the Colorado River.

Ironjaw Canyon

Tough, raw, and unforgettable — Ironjaw Canyon is not for the faint of heart. This advanced run pushes you through roaring class IV rapids framed by towering granite walls. Between runs, catch your breath on calm stretches where the canyon opens into brilliant blue sky. Seasoned paddlers call it “the ride of a lifetime.”
